About


Lirang-lirang Beach is a hidden gem known for its tranquility and unspoiled natural beauty. Its 100-step staircase nestled between mountains and the sea creates a serene and peaceful atmosphere, perfect for families and friends seeking relaxation and bonding.


Information


Lirang-lirang Beach resort is located at Barangay,Tolibao Capoocan Leyte ,From Tacloban,you'll have to take 1 and half hour to ride.Lirang-lirang Beach is a hidden gem that promises tranquility and unspoiled natural beauty.

Lirang Lirang beach is known for their 100 steps stairs nestled between mountains and the sea, providing a serene and peaceful atmosphere. This is perfect for families and friends looking for a place to bond and relax.

Entrance fee is 10 PHP, while the cottages range from 250-300 PHP, Adult life vests are available for 30 PHP and a floating cottage can be rented for 750 PHP

When planning a visit to Lirang-Lirang Beach, it's important to note that parking options are somewhat limited. There is no designated parking lot available, which encourages visitors to find street parking nearby. This can sometimes be a challenge, especially during peak seasons when the beach becomes a popular destination for both locals and tourists. Consequently, guests are advised to arrive early to secure a parking spot and consider carpooling to minimize parking difficulties.
